Welcome to the Official Profile of DJ El Djado

DJ El Djado is a Paris-based DJ, renowned for his ability to blend Afro and Hip-Hop sounds to create a unique and captivating atmosphere. With several years of experience, he has made a name for himself on the Parisian music scene and beyond. His expertise is not limited to just one aspect of musical entertainment: he is also specialized in hosting various events, including weddings, baptisms, and nightclub parties.
